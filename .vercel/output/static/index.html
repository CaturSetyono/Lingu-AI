Frequently Asked Questions

▶ Is my text data stored anywhere?

No. LinguAI Studio respects your privacy. All processing happens in your browser or device. We never store, log, or transmit your text data to any server.

▶ How much does it cost?

LinguAI Studio is completely free. No hidden charges, no premium features, no sign-up required. Use it as much as you want.

▶ Can I use this offline?

Yes. LinguAI Studio is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it on your device and use it offline. Perfect for when you don't have internet connection.

▶ What languages are supported?

We support English, Indonesian, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and more. The AI can understand and rewrite in multiple languages while maintaining quality and accuracy.
